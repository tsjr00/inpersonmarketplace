# Competitor Scan — Tier 1 (Direct: preorder + pickup / market-day fulfillment)

> Focus: platforms that look most like the **InPerson Marketplace** flow (buyers pre-order, sellers fulfill for in-person pickup; often multi-vendor, market-host, or scheduled pickup).

---

## Locally Grown
- **Website:** https://www.locallygrown.app/
- **What they do:** “pre-orders for pickup or delivery” + lightweight POS for farms/market days.
- **Why it’s a direct competitor:** mirrors your “paid ahead + show up with the right inventory + pickup flow.”
- **Notable workflow/features:** pre-orders, pickup/delivery locations, Stripe payout, market-day POS.
- **Public contact / support:** ja@locallygrown.app (listed in terms/blog); Location listed as Raleigh, NC (blog footer).
- **Sources:**  
  - Terms mention contact email: https://www.locallygrown.app/terms  
  - Blog footer lists Raleigh, NC + email: https://locallygrown.app/blog/2025-06-16-locally-grown-is-growing-with-support-from-the-usda

## WhatsGood (SourceWhatsGood)
- **Website:** https://sourcewhatsgood.com/

## Soko (Farmers Market App by Ujama Inc.)
