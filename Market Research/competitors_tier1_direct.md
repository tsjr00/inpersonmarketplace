# Competitor Scan — Tier 1 (Direct: preorder + pickup / market-day fulfillment)

> Focus: platforms that look most like the **InPerson Marketplace** flow (buyers pre-order, sellers fulfill for in-person pickup; often multi-vendor, market-host, or scheduled pickup).

---

## Locally Grown
- **Website:** https://www.locallygrown.app/
- **What they do:** “pre-orders for pickup or delivery” + lightweight POS for farms/market days.
- **Why it’s a direct competitor:** mirrors your “paid ahead + show up with the right inventory + pickup flow.”
- **Notable workflow/features:** pre-orders, pickup/delivery locations, Stripe payout, market-day POS.
- **Public contact / support:** ja@locallygrown.app (listed in terms/blog); Location listed as Raleigh, NC (blog footer).
- **Sources:**  
  - Terms mention contact email: https://www.locallygrown.app/terms  
  - Blog footer lists Raleigh, NC + email: https://locallygrown.app/blog/2025-06-16-locally-grown-is-growing-with-support-from-the-usda

## Homegrown
- **Website:** https://findhomegrown.com/
- **What they do:** “Your Online Farmers Market” (pre-order for pickup at local markets / nearby farms & artisans).
- **Why it’s a direct competitor:** same buyer behavior (browse vendors → pre-order → pickup).
- **Public contact / support:** help@findhomegrown.com.
- **Sources:**  
  - Privacy/Terms contact email: https://findhomegrown.com/legal/privacy (also /legal/terms)

## FarmDrop (FarmDrop L3C)
- **Website:** https://farmdrop.us/
- **What they do:** online ordering for local farms with **pickup locations** (market-hub model).
- **Why it’s a direct competitor:** multi-producer ordering + pickup windows.
- **Public contact / support:** info@farmdrop.us; Phone: 812-361-6577; Mailing: PO Box 292, Blue Hill, ME 04614; Entity: FarmDrop L3C.
- **Sources:**  
  - Terms & Conditions contact block: https://farmdrop.us/terms-and-conditions/

## Market Wagon (Market Wagon LLC)
- **Website:** https://www.marketwagon.com/
- **What they do:** online marketplace for local food; fulfillment by delivery and “market host” pickup concepts.
- **Why it’s a direct competitor:** multi-vendor cart + logistics layer (they solve “what will I sell?” and “how do buyers get it?”).
- **Public info (founder):** founded 2017 by Nick Carter and Dan Brunner (about/mission copy).
- **Public contact:** Phone + address appear on public business listings (not prominently on their site).
- **Sources:**  
  - Mission/founding paragraph: https://www.marketwagon.com/  
  - Public listing with phone/address: https://www.yelp.com/biz/market-wagon-indianapolis

## Fellow Farmer (NYC-focused “farmers market delivery / preorder” concept)
- **Website:** https://fellowfarmer.com/
- **What they do:** preorder from farmers market producers; order is picked/packed from stands and delivered (market-runner model).
- **Why it’s a direct competitor:** extremely close to “market-day order aggregation,” just with delivery instead of pickup.
- **Public contact (related org “Fellow Farmers”):** director contact is public on fellowfarmers.live (note: different domain/brand; verify relationship before outreach).
- **Sources:**  
  - Product positioning (homepage): https://fellowfarmer.com/  
  - Contact page (Fellow Farmers): https://fellowfarmers.live/contact/

## Market Day Local (fundraiser pickup model)
- **Website:** https://www.marketdaylocal.com/
- **What they do:** online ordering with scheduled pickup; oriented toward fundraising groups (schools/churches/non-profits).
- **Why it’s a direct competitor:** same transaction pattern (pre-order → in-person pickup window), but different vertical.
- **Public contact:** help@marketdaylocal.com.
- **Sources:**  
  - Contact page: https://www.marketdaylocal.com/pages/contact
