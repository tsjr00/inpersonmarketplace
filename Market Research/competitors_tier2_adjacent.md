# Competitor Scan — Tier 2 (Adjacent: farm commerce + multi-vendor + CSA/food hub operations)

> Focus: solutions that can be substituted for parts of your product (online store, CSA scheduling, multi-vendor payouts, pickup/delivery plans), even if they aren’t “market-day preorder” first.
