Buyers are customers who browse, purchase, and pick up products from local vendors at farmers markets and private pickup locations.

## Browse & Discovery

The platform makes it easy to find fresh, local products from verified vendors in your area.

### Product Browsing
- **Grid-based product display** shows listings with images, prices, and vendor information at a glance
- **Dual view toggle** lets you switch between individual product listings and 4-week Market Box subscriptions
- **Category filtering** narrows results to specific product types like produce, baked goods, dairy, or meats
- **Location-based search** filters products by ZIP code proximity so you see what's available near you
- **Real-time availability indicators** show stock levels and whether items are still available to order

### Vendor Discovery
- **Vendor directory** displays all approved vendors with their ratings, tier badges, and product categories
- **Market filtering** shows which vendors sell at specific farmers markets
- **Ratings and reviews** help you identify quality vendors through community feedback
- **Member-since dates** indicate how long vendors have been active on the platform
- **Tier badges** (Standard, Premium, Featured) show vendor commitment level

### Market Discovery
- **Market listings page** shows all approved farmers markets with addresses and operating schedules
- **City filtering** helps you find markets in your specific area
- **Vendor counts** indicate how many sellers are at each market location
- **Schedule information** displays days and hours of operation for planning your visits

## Shopping Experience

A streamlined shopping flow from product selection through checkout.

### Product Details
- **Multi-image galleries** with zoom let you examine products closely before purchasing
- **Detailed descriptions** provide complete product information including ingredients and preparation methods
- **Allergen warnings** are prominently displayed with badge indicators for items containing common allergens
- **Price and quantity display** shows unit pricing and available stock clearly
- **Vendor information card** links to the seller's profile and other products they offer

### Pickup Location Information
- **Pickup locations card** shows all markets and private locations where a product can be collected
- **Real-time order status** indicates whether each location is currently accepting orders
- **Cutoff countdowns** display how much time remains before orders close for each pickup window
- **Schedule details** show exact dates and times for each pickup opportunity
- **Color-coded status** uses green (accepting), yellow (closing soon), and red (closed) for quick recognition

### Cart Management
- **Persistent cart** saves your selections across browser sessions so you never lose your items
- **Quick add buttons** let you add products to cart directly from browse and listing pages
- **Slide-out cart drawer** provides easy access to review and modify your cart without leaving the page
- **Quantity adjustments** allow you to increase, decrease, or remove items with simple controls
- **Multi-location alerts** notify you when your cart contains items from different pickup locations

### Checkout Process
- **Payment method selection** lets you choose from multiple options:
  - *Credit/Debit Card* ‚Äî Processed securely through Stripe
  - *Venmo* ‚Äî Peer-to-peer payment through the Venmo app
  - *Cash App* ‚Äî Mobile payment through Cash App
  - *PayPal* ‚Äî Online payment via PayPal.me

## Order Management

Track your orders from purchase through pickup with full visibility into every step.

### Order Tracking
- **Order list view** shows all your orders with current status and key details
- **Multi-stage status tracking** keeps you informed through the fulfillment process:
  - *Order Placed* ‚Äî Your order has been submitted and payment confirmed
  - *Confirmed* ‚Äî The vendor has accepted your order and will prepare it
  - *Ready for Pickup* ‚Äî Your items are prepared and waiting for you
  - *Acknowledge Receipt* ‚Äî Confirm you've received your items at pickup
  - *Completed* ‚Äî Transaction fully completed
- **Order detail pages** provide complete breakdowns including items, quantities, prices, and pickup information
- **Visual timeline** displays the progression of events for each order

### Pickup Confirmation
- **Two-step verification** protects both buyers and vendors during handoff:
  1. You acknowledge receipt of your items on your device
  2. The vendor confirms the handoff within 30 seconds on their device
- **Secure confirmation window** ensures both parties are present for the exchange
- **Digital receipt** records the confirmed pickup for your records

### Issue Resolution
- **Problem reporting** lets you flag issues with missing or incorrect items
- **Item selection** allows you to specify exactly which products had problems
- **Description field** provides space to explain the issue in detail
- **Vendor notification** automatically alerts the seller to investigate and resolve

### Cancellation & Refunds
- **Order cancellation** is available for orders that haven't been prepared yet
- **Refund tracking** shows the status and amount of any refunds processed
- **Clear policies** explain cancellation windows and refund timelines

- **Buyer fee (external payments):** 6.5% + $0.15 per transaction
