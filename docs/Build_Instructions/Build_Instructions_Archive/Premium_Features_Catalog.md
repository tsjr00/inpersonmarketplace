# Premium Features Catalog

This document catalogs all premium features for buyers and vendors. Use this list for:
- Upgrade page benefits display
- Downgrade confirmation warnings
- Marketing materials

---

## PREMIUM SHOPPER MEMBERSHIP

### Pricing
- **Monthly**: $9.99/month
- **Annual**: $81.50/year (Save 32% - effectively $6.79/month)

---

### Feature List (Display on Upgrade Page)

#### Market Box Subscriptions
1. **Exclusive Access to Market Box Subscriptions**
   - Only premium shoppers can purchase Market Boxes
   - 4-week prepaid subscription bundles from your favorite vendors
   - Weekly pickups guaranteed for the full subscription period

2. **Subscribe to Multiple Market Boxes**
   - Build your weekly routine with boxes from different vendors
   - Never miss out on limited-availability seasonal items

3. **Priority Access to Limited Market Boxes**
   - Premium vendors offer unlimited subscriber spots to premium shoppers
   - Standard vendor boxes limited to 2 subscribers - premium gets priority

#### Early Access & Discovery
4. **Early Access to New Listings**
   - See new products before standard shoppers
   - Get first pick on limited quantity items

5. **Early Access to Seasonal Products**
   - Be first to know when seasonal favorites become available
   - Reserve items before they sell out

#### Support & Service
6. **Priority Customer Support**
   - Faster response times on all inquiries
   - Dedicated support for questions or issues

7. **Direct Support Channel**
   - Get help resolving order issues quickly
   - Priority handling for time-sensitive matters

#### Analytics & Tracking
8. **Order Insights Dashboard**
   - Track your purchase history over time
   - See spending trends and patterns

9. **Purchase History Analytics**
   - View your favorite vendors and products
   - Track how often you buy from each vendor

10. **Favorites Management**
    - Save and organize your favorite products
    - Quick reorder from your favorites list

#### Recognition & Community
11. **Premium Shopper Badge**
    - Display badge on your profile
    - Show your support for local vendors

12. **Support Local Agriculture**
    - Your membership helps sustain the platform
    - Direct support for local farmers and producers

---

### What You Lose If You Downgrade (Show on Downgrade Confirmation)

- Access to Market Box subscriptions
- Early access to new listings
- Priority customer support
- Order insights and analytics
- Premium shopper badge
- Priority access to limited items
- Benefits active until end of billing period
- No refunds for unused subscription time

---

## PREMIUM VENDOR MEMBERSHIP

### Pricing
- **Monthly**: $24.99/month
- **Annual**: $208.15/year (Save 30% - effectively $17.35/month)

---

### Feature List (Display on Upgrade Page)

#### Product Listings
1. **10 Active Product Listings**
   - List up to 10 products (vs. 5 for standard)
   - More products = more sales opportunities

2. **Double Your Product Catalog**
   - Twice the listings of standard vendors
   - Showcase your full range of products

3. **No Listing Limits on Bundles**
   - Create product bundles without counting against limits
   - Package deals to increase order value

#### Market & Location Access
4. **Sell at 4 Traditional Markets**
   - Expand to 4 farmers markets (vs. 1 for standard)
   - Reach more customers across different locations

5. **Multiple Market Presence**
   - Don't be limited to a single "home market"
   - Build your brand across the region

6. **5 Private Pickup Locations**
   - Set up 5 private pickup spots (vs. 1 for standard)
   - Offer farm pickup, delivery points, and more

7. **Flexible Pickup Options**
   - More locations = more convenience for customers
   - Accommodate different neighborhoods and schedules

8. **6 Pickup Windows Per Location**
   - Set 6 time slots per location (vs. 2 for standard)
   - Morning, afternoon, evening options

9. **Extended Pickup Hours**
   - Offer more flexibility to your customers
   - Capture sales from different schedules

#### Market Box Subscriptions
10. **Create 6 Market Box Offerings**
    - Offer up to 6 different subscription boxes (vs. 2 for standard)
    - Variety keeps customers engaged

11. **4 Active Market Boxes Simultaneously**
    - Run 4 subscription programs at once (vs. 1 for standard)
    - Seasonal boxes, weekly staples, specialty boxes

12. **Unlimited Market Box Subscribers**
    - No cap on how many customers can subscribe
    - Standard vendors limited to 2 subscribers per box

13. **Guaranteed Recurring Revenue**
    - 4-week prepaid subscriptions = predictable income
    - Plan production around committed orders

14. **Build Customer Loyalty**
    - Subscription customers return week after week
    - Develop lasting relationships with your buyers

#### Search & Visibility
15. **Priority Placement in Search Results**
    - Your listings appear higher in search
    - More visibility = more clicks = more sales

16. **Featured on Homepage**
    - Get highlighted on the homepage
    - Prime real estate for new customer discovery

17. **Featured in Browse Pages**
    - Stand out in category and browse pages
    - Catch the eye of browsing shoppers

18. **Featured in Market Pages**
    - Highlighted when customers browse by market
    - More exposure at each location you sell

#### Branding & Recognition
19. **Premium Vendor Badge**
    - Display badge on your vendor profile
    - Build trust and credibility with shoppers

20. **Premium Badge on All Listings**
    - Every product shows your premium status
    - Stand out from standard vendors

21. **Enhanced Profile Display**
    - Premium vendors get enhanced profile styling
    - Professional appearance builds confidence

#### Analytics & Insights
22. **Advanced Analytics Dashboard**
    - Detailed sales and performance insights
    - Make data-driven decisions

23. **Sales Trends Over Time**
    - View revenue trends by day, week, month
    - Identify your best-performing periods

24. **Top Products Report**
    - See which products sell best
    - Focus on what customers want

25. **Customer Insights**
    - Track new vs. returning customers
    - Understand your customer base

26. **Order Status Analytics**
    - Breakdown of completed, pending, cancelled orders
    - Identify and fix issues quickly

27. **Average Order Value Tracking**
    - Monitor basket size over time
    - Strategies to increase order value

---

### What You Lose If You Downgrade (Show on Downgrade Confirmation)

#### Listing Limits
- Product listings reduced to 5 (excess will be unpublished)
- You must choose which 5 products to keep active

#### Market Access
- Limited to 1 traditional market only
- Must select your "home market" - others removed
- Private pickup locations reduced to 1
- Pickup windows reduced to 2 per location

#### Market Box Restrictions
- Market boxes reduced to 2 total
- Only 1 active market box allowed
- Subscriber limit drops to 2 per box
- Existing subscribers over limit cannot renew

#### Visibility Loss
- No more priority search placement
- Removed from featured sections
- No more homepage highlights
- Premium badge removed from profile and listings

#### Analytics Access
- Advanced analytics dashboard no longer accessible
- Sales trends and insights unavailable
- Customer analytics removed

#### Important Notes
- Benefits remain active until end of billing period
- No refunds for unused annual subscription time
- Excess listings/markets/boxes will be automatically managed
