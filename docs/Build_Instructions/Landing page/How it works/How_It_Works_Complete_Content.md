# SECTION 1: FOR BUYERS

## Welcome to Farm-Direct Shopping

**You're about to discover a better way to buy fresh, local food.**

Skip the grocery store. Skip the crowds. Skip the uncertainty of "will they have what I want?"

With our platform, you browse local products from your phone, order what you want, and pick it up fresh at your neighborhood farmers market‚Äîguaranteed to be there, set aside just for you.

**This is how shopping for local food should work.**

---

## Your Shopping Journey (7 Steps)

### Step 1: Browse Local Products

**What you do:** Explore products from vendors at markets near you.

**What you'll see:**
- Farm-fresh produce, eggs, honey, baked goods, and more
- Photos and descriptions from real local producers
- Pickup locations and dates for each item
- Transparent pricing (what you see is what you pay)

**Why this matters:**  
You're seeing exactly what local farmers and artisans are bringing to your market this week. No surprises, no sold-out disappointments.

**Benefits to you:**
- Shop from your couch, not in a parking lot
- Compare options across multiple vendors
- Plan your market trip in advance

**Benefits to local food:**  
Your pre-orders help small producers plan their harvests and reduce food waste. They bring exactly what's ordered, nothing gets thrown away at day's end.

---

### Step 2: Add Items to Your Cart

**What you do:** Select items from one or more vendors. Add to cart.

**The rule:** Minimum $10 order (combined total across all vendors).

**Why this minimum exists:**  
Small orders cost us the same to process, but vendors receive very little after payment processing fees. The $10 minimum ensures farmers and artisans get fair compensation for their work.

**Benefits to you:**
- Encourages trying products from multiple vendors
- Discover variety you wouldn't find at a grocery store
- Build relationships with several local producers

**Benefits to local food:**  
When you support multiple vendors in one trip, you strengthen the entire local food ecosystem. Markets thrive when shoppers explore beyond just one favorite booth.

---

### Step 3: Secure Checkout

**What you do:** Complete payment with your credit card.

**What happens:**
- Payment is processed immediately (secure, encrypted)
- Order is sent to each vendor
- You receive confirmation email with order details
- Money is held safely until you pick up your items

**Why we hold payment:**  
This protects you. Vendors don't get paid until they hand you your items and you confirm you received them. If something goes wrong, you're covered.

**Pricing transparency:**
- Vendor sets base price (what they need to earn)
- Platform adds 6.5% + $0.15 service fee
- You see total price at checkout (no hidden fees)

**Benefits to you:**
- Your payment is protected
- No cash needed at the market
- Digital receipt for your records
- Guaranteed your items will be there

**Benefits to local food:**  
Pre-payment allows small producers to invest in growing and preparing your order with confidence. No financial risk for them means more farmers can participate.

---

### Step 4: Vendor Confirms Your Order

**What happens (within 24 hours):**
- Vendor reviews your order
- Confirms they can fulfill it
- Begins preparing your items

**You'll receive:** Email notification that your order is confirmed.

**Why this step exists:**  
Farmers work with nature‚Äîweather, crop yields, and livestock production aren't always predictable. This confirmation ensures the vendor can actually deliver what you ordered.

**Benefits to you:**
- No last-minute surprises
- Vendor has time to ask questions if needed
- You know your order is coming

**Benefits to local food:**  
This honest communication builds trust. Farmers aren't pressured to promise what they can't deliver. You get realistic expectations, not false hopes.

---

### Step 5: Items Marked Ready for Pickup

**What happens (before market day):**
- Vendor harvests, bakes, or prepares your items fresh
- Marks your order "Ready for Pickup" in the system
- Your items are labeled with your order number

**You'll receive:** Email and app notification that your order is ready.

**When to pick up:** Visit the market on the scheduled pickup day/time shown in your order.

**Benefits to you:**
- Items prepared fresh, not sitting in storage
- No guessing if your order is ready
- Vendor expects you (no awkward "do you have my order?" moments)

**Benefits to local food:**  
Just-in-time preparation means maximum freshness. Your tomatoes were picked that morning, your bread came out of the oven hours ago. This is farm-direct at its best.

---

### Step 6: Pick Up at the Market ‚≠ê **MOST IMPORTANT STEP**

**What you do:**
1. Visit the market on your scheduled pickup day
2. Find the vendor's booth (location in your order details)
3. Open your order in the app ‚Üí Tap **"I'm Here to Pick Up"**
4. Show your order number to the vendor
5. Vendor verifies and hands you your items
6. Check that you received everything

**Then‚Äîthis is critical:**  
**Tap "Yes, I Received Everything" in your app**

**What happens next:**
- Vendor receives notification on their phone
- They tap "Yes, I Handed It Off" (within 30 seconds)
- Both screens turn green ‚úÖ
- This triggers payment to the vendor

**Why both parties confirm:**  
This is the digital handshake. It protects you (vendor can't claim they delivered if they didn't) and protects the vendor (you can't claim you didn't receive items if you did). Both confirmations happen face-to-face, standing at the booth together.

**IMPORTANT: Don't walk away until both of you see green screens.**

This 30-second confirmation ensures:
- You actually received your items
- Vendor actually handed them off
- Payment happens fairly and transparently
- No disputes about "who got what"

**Benefits to you:**
- Payment doesn't go to vendor until you confirm
- If items are missing/wrong, you can report the issue instead of confirming
- You're protected from vendor no-shows
- Clear accountability

**Benefits to vendors:**
- They get paid immediately after honest handoff
- No risk of customers falsely claiming non-delivery
- Clear proof of transaction completion

**Benefits to local food:**  
Trust is everything in direct-to-consumer relationships. This confirmation system builds mutual accountability, making farmers markets safer for everyone‚Äîbuyers, vendors, and the platform that connects you.

**What you do:** Take your items home and enjoy!

**What happens behind the scenes:**

- You can rate your experience

**Why this matters:**  
Every purchase directly supports a local producer. Your money goes to someone in your community who grows, bakes, or makes real food with their own hands.
