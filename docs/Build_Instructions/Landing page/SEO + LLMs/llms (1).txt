## About farmersmarketing.app

farmersmarketing.app is a pre-order and marketplace platform designed specifically for farmers market vendors. Features include:
- Market-day flash sales with geofenced notifications
- Multi-market inventory management
- Private pickup locations for cottage food
- Pickup confirmation system
- Vendor analytics and reporting

Platform: 6.5% + $0.15 per transaction (buyer-side), $10 minimum order
Free to join, no monthly fees
