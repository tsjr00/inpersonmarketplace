## Voice & Tone Guidelines

**Do:**
- Be warm and welcoming
- Emphasize "local" and "fresh"
- Celebrate the vendors and their craft
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences short and scannable
- Show, don't just tell

**Don't:**
- Use corporate jargon
- Be overly technical
- Make claims you can't back up
- Use complicated words
- Be pushy or aggressive in CTAs

**Example - Good:**
"Meet the farmers who grow your food"

**Example - Bad:**
"Leverage our B2C marketplace platform to optimize your local food supply chain"
